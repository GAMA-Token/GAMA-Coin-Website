    FAQ's

    Frequently Asked Questions
    What is GAMA Token?
    GAMA Token is a Web3 gaming token used within the GAMA platform to participate in games, place bets, and earn rewards. Build on XDC Network.
    How does the GAMA platform generate rewards?
    The platform rewards players by generating additional token supply, which is distributed based on game outcomes and player performance.
    What happens if a player loses a bet or game?
    If a player loses, the GAMA tokens used in the bet are burned, reducing the total supply and potentially increasing the value of remaining tokens.
    How are new games added to the GAMA platform?
    New games are added based on community demand. The GAMA platform regularly reviews player feedback and adds games that align with user interests.
    Can I trade GAMA tokens outside the platform?
    Yes, GAMA tokens can be traded on supported decentralized exchanges like XSWAP, providing liquidity and market opportunities for players.
    How secure is the GAMA platform?
    The GAMA platform leverages blockchain technology of XDC Network and smart contracts to ensure transparency, security, and fairness in all gaming activities.
    How can I participate in the GAMA community?
    Players can join the GAMA community through various social media channels, participate in discussions, and contribute to the development of new games and features.
